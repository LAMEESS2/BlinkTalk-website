About BlinkTalk

BlinkTalk is a groundbreaking assistive communication system that transforms the simple act of blinking into a powerful means of expression. Designed for individuals with limited mobility or speech impairments, our system uses advanced computer vision and Morse code to enable communication, independence, and connection.

By combining real-time eye-blink detection with IoT connectivity, BlinkTalk creates a bridge between those who struggle to communicate and the world around them. Our low-cost, accessible solution empowers users to express needs, send messages, make calls, and interact with their environment—all through intentional blinks.

How It Works

Three simple steps to enable communication

01

Blink Detection

Advanced computer vision algorithms accurately detect and differentiate between intentional blinks and natural eye movements in real-time.

  • Left/right eye distinction
  • Duration tracking
  • Adjustable sensitivity
02

Morse Translation

Blinks are instantly processed and translated into Morse code, then decoded into letters, numbers, and commands with high accuracy.

  • Dot & dash recognition
  • Character decoding
  • Error correction
03

Communication Output

Decoded messages are converted into speech, text messages, or phone calls, enabling multi-channel communication with the outside world.

  • Text-to-speech
  • SMS messaging
  • Voice calls
